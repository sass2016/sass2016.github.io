About

Welcome to ADMIyou! We are Sofia Belimova, Sarah Chowdhury, Sofia Creanza, and Amandi Perera. We are part of the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in Brooklyn and this our final project.

We made this website to spread awareness about the effects of negative body image. Our website is aimed toward adolescents struggling with self esteem issues. We want to teach people about perception of body image and how to appreciate themselves.

According to the National Institute of Mental Health, more than 5 million Americans are affected by eating disorders every year. From those 5 million, about 1,000 women die each year from anorexia nervosa. Many resources for adolescents that address body image target girls, but it is important to remember that girls are not the only ones who struggle with this issue. Our website is aimed toward all young people and encourages them to accept themselves for the way they are.
